
# LifeOS - Ultimate Habit & Career Tracker


> **Your complete personal operating system. Track habits, journal daily, manage career roadmaps, and stay motivated—all in one beautiful, offline-first web app.**

## Features

###  Habit Tracking
- **GitHub-style contribution graph** - Visualize your consistency over 365 days
- **Smart streak calculation** - Never lose motivation with accurate streak tracking
- **Category-based habits** - Organize by Health, Career, Learning, Creative, Social
- **Time travel** - Click any past day to see exactly what you accomplished

###  Daily Journal
- **Date-stamped entries** - Every thought saved to its specific day
- **Character counter** - Know when you've written enough
- **Export functionality** - Backup your memories as JSON
- **Read-only history** - Browse past entries without accidentally editing

###  Media & Motivation
- **YouTube integration** - Paste any URL to play motivational videos, lo-fi beats, or tutorials
- **Personal playlists** - Save videos to specific dates for future reference
- **Local video support** - Three built-in motivational videos for offline use
- **Privacy-first** - Uses youtube-nocookie.com for enhanced privacy

###  Career Roadmap
- **Milestone tracking** - Break big goals into achievable steps
- **Category progress bars** - Visualize Academic, Career, and Personal growth
- **Import/Export roadmaps** - Share plans or backup your strategy
- **Active goals sidebar** - Always see your next 3 priorities

###  Personalization
- **Profile photos** - Upload your avatar (stored locally)
- **Custom bio & name** - Make it truly yours
- **Daily reminders** - Set your preferred notification time
- **Responsive design** - Works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop
